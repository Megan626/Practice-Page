Fourth Layer of the Ocean
Abyssopelagic/Abyssal Zone



- Depths of 9,800 to 19,700 ft
- Largest environment for earth life- spanning about 115,000,000 square miles
- Takes up more than 83% of the ocean and covers about 60% of Earth
- Low energy- far from storms on the surface
- No sunlight, close to freezing temperatures, high pressure (11,000 pounds per inch)
- Creatures that live in this zone: sea cucumbers, algae, flower basket sponge, tripod fish, brittle star, dumbo octopus anglerfish, and squid
